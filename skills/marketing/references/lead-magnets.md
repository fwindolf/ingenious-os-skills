# Lead Magnets

Creating gated content for email capture.

## Principles

- Solve a specific problem for our ICP
- Match buyer stage
- High perceived value + low time investment
- Natural path to ingeniOS
- Easy to consume (one format)

## Formats by Effort

| Format | Creation Time | Best For |
|--------|--------------|----------|
| Checklist | 1-2 hrs | Quick wins, compliance |
| Cheat sheet | 2-4 hrs | Reference material |
| Template | 2-8 hrs | Practical tools |
| Guide/Ebook | 1-3 weeks | Deep education |
| Quiz/Assessment | 1-2 weeks | Qualification |

## Ideas for Handwerk ICP

- "Die 5 häufigsten Fehler bei der Zeiterfassung im Handwerk"

**Decision stage**:
- "Angebots-Vorlage: SHK-Betrieb (ZUGFeRD-konform)"

## Landing Page Structure

1. Headline (what they get)
2. Preview/Mockup image
3. What's inside (3-5 bullets)
4. Social proof if available
5. Form (email only for highest conversion — each extra field reduces conversion 5-10%)
6. FAQ

## Form Strategy

- Email only = highest conversion
- Add fields only if needed for qualification
- Every field reduces conversion 5-10%

## Metrics

- Landing page conversion: 20-40% (warm traffic), 5-15% (cold)
- Lead-to-customer rate: 1-5% (B2B)
- Email engagement: 30-50% open, 2-5% click
